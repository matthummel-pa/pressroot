How it works

Three steps. No page builder. No lock-in.

Like Elementor's outcome without Elementor's overhead — the page builder is WordPress itself, and AI does the assembling.

1

Tell it your brand

Name, one-liner, industry, audience, voice, goal, imagery, density — plus free-form AI instructions and uploadable .md brand docs. This becomes the core brief behind every AI call.

2

Save — it builds

One save compiles the brief, deals a design (kit + trend filtered by your answers), generates pages on core Gutenberg blocks, writes the copy, creates images, and wires nav, header CTA, and footer.

3

Refresh until you love it

Every 🎲 deals a genuinely different site — new palette, fonts, layouts, and design trend that never repeats the current one. Your brand color and content survive every deal.

Features

A design system that designs, and an AI that writes.

✨

AI is the page builder

Pages are plain Gutenberg blocks the owner can edit like any other page. The AI writes the copy and generates the images — it never touches your markup, so nothing ever breaks.

🎨

Design generator

13 style kits × 6 design trends × randomized layout variants, filtered by your brand answers. Every refresh is a coherent, complete new theme — not a reskin.

🧭

Full site chrome

Builds don't stop at content: a generated nav menu, a goal-driven header CTA (Book now / Shop now / Get a quote / Subscribe), and a brand-driven footer ship with every site.

💸

Free AI by default

Works out of the box with keyless free models for text and images. Bring your own key for Claude, GPT, Gemini, or Groq when you want more — with automatic free fallback.

🏪

8 site types

Restaurant, Realty, Affiliate, Agency, Freelance, SaaS, Blog, Marketing — each with starter pages, industry questions the AI treats as facts, and matched design pools.

🐙

Repofolio addon

Live GitHub portfolio pages built in: connect a profile and ship project pages with real stats, releases, and issues. Perfect for developer sites.
